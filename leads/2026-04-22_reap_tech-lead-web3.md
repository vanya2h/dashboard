# Reap — Senior Software Engineer / Tech Lead, Web3 Initiatives

## Job Description

> Source: https://jobstash.xyz/senior-software-engineer-tech-lead-web3-initiatives-reap/ghHgLz

Reap is building card-linked stablecoin and crypto payment infrastructure — you own the entire web3 layer. The role covers wallet infrastructure discovery and development for the Card product, production-grade smart contract deployment across Solana and Ethereum, and backend services in Node.js/TypeScript supporting on-chain functionality.

**Responsibilities:**

- Own Reap's web3 capabilities end-to-end — from architecture to deployment
- Design and deploy production-grade smart contracts on Solana (Rust) and Ethereum (Solidity)
- Build backend services in Node.js and TypeScript to support on-chain functionality
- Design and ship wallet infrastructure — hot wallets, cold wallets, stablecoin rails
- Collaborate with product and business teams to translate ideas into secure technical execution
- Contribute to architecture decisions, code reviews, and security audits
- Leverage AI tools and modern practices to increase engineering efficiency

**Requirements:**

- 5–8+ years in software development with backend engineering in Node.js/TypeScript and AWS (or similar cloud)
- 3+ years building smart contracts on Solana (Rust) or Ethereum (Solidity)
- Experience building wallet infrastructure (hot and cold wallets)
- Strong product sense and ability to take end-to-end ownership

**Skills listed:** Multi-Chain, Hot Wallet, Solana, Cross-Chain, Ethereum, Node.js, TypeScript, Architecture, DeFi, AWS, Backend, Cold Storage, Rust, Solidity, Payments, Smart Contract, Security Audit, Card Issuance, Wallet Infrastructure, Cold Wallet, Stablecoin, AI
